Food & Hygiene Assistance

Northside’s Midtown Food Pantry:
If you have a need for food, the Midtown Food Pantry offers drive-thru service on Tues/Thur evenings from 5:30-6:30pm, at 1423 E Oak Street, New Albany. The pantry provides food and hygiene products for people who need it. Clients can receive assistance once per calendar month. You do not need to fill out the form to pick up food from Midtown, only a photo ID.
