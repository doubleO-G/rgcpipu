Food Pantry

Error

Tue. & Thu. 5:30-6:30PM

Midtown Food Pantry
1423 East Oak Street
New Albany, IN 47150
PHONE: (812) 542-4079

Northside's Midtown Food Pantry helps feed over 5,000 people per year. It's open twice a week to anyone in need of food. Each person or family is allowed to come once a month to choose items from our Food Pantry shopping list. When available, we can also provide baby items and Bibles.

In need of food?

If you are in need of food assistance please visit the pantry on Tuesday or Thursday, 5:30-6:30pm. You will need to bring a photo id and 1 piece of mail showing your current address.

Interested in volunteering?

Volunteers are always welcome and needed Tue. & Thur. to help run the Food Pantry services. This ministry is a great opportunity for families of all ages to serve together, pick up food donations, and re-stock the pantry.

Serve

Food Donation

Throughout the year, Northside's Food Pantry needs donated non-perishable grocery items that will be donated to local families in need. To help, simply pick up a green recyclable grocery bag located near Northside's entrances, scan the QR code to see the items needed, and return it to Northside.
