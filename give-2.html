Your generosity helps us continue our mission of connecting unconnected people to Jesus Christ.

How to Get Started

It's simple, secure, and best of all, only requires 30 seconds to complete. To start the process, please choose your giving option below.  Personal information will be requested in order to accurately record your donation and capture the information required for tax purposes.  You will need to click "Sign Up" if this is your first visit to the repeat giving area.

One-time Gift Your gift will be given to the church immediately.
Repeating Gift Your gift will be given to the church as often as you choose.

Giving Statements will be sent electronically the last week of January if we have your email address on file. If we do not have an email address, we will mail those statements. Those sent electronically will show coming from Pushpay.

Questions?

If you have any questions or want further information on Northside's Automated Giving program, please email us at info@mynorthside.com or call (812) 945-8704.
