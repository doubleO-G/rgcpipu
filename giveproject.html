WHAT IS THE GIVE PROJECT?

Every other month on a single weekend, Northside takes the cash offering dropped in the giving drop boxes on campus and gives it to a local or global outreach partner to fill a specific need. Through the Give Project, we've seen the collective generosity of the Norhside family make an incredible difference in the lives of people here in South Indiana and around the world.
