Next Steps

YOU ARE SO WELCOME HERE!

Error

We believe every follower of Jesus has a next step, and we hope we can help you take yours!


Are you...

  • New to Northside?
  • Want to learn more about how to get connected?
  • Want to take your next step, but not sure where to start?
  • Interested in learning more about the mission and vision of Northside?

Then Next Steps is for you!

Next Steps is a two-week gathering for those who are new or want to get more connected at Northside. We gather during the 11:30 AM service in the West Auditorium. Brunch is provided the first week, and lunch provided the second week. Registration is only required once. Children's Ministry services are provided through our regular service Kidside check-in. Please reach out if you have any questions!
