General Serving

If you are new to Northside or haven't yet taken the step of serving and you aren't quite sure where you'd like to serve, connect with us here and we will walk you through some of the serving opportunities to help you find the best fit!

Our First Impressions Team exists to create a warm environment and make everyone feel welcome and at home at Northside!

Family Ministries

A win for those serving in our children’s ministry begins with helping a child to feel welcome at church and ultimately leads to them feeling connected to Jesus and their friends.

More Info

Community Outreach

Serving the community through Local Outreach provides you with the opportunity to partner with God to restore hope to our community.

Administrative

Our Volunteer Receptionists are an integral part of making the office of Northside Christian Church run smoothly and efficiently on a daily basis.

More Info

Groups

Serving as a Group leader is all about connecting with others, and helping people take next steps of faith through community.

More Info

Health and Safety

Serving on HST is a behind-the-scenes opportunity to help create a distraction-free and safe environment for everyone at Northside during the weekend services and special events. 

More Info

Facilities

Serving with the Facilities Team involves working with a fun group of people that prepare the building and grounds to give our Northside family a warm welcome.

More Info

Care Ministry

Serving in Care Ministry means getting to walk with people as they are spiritually transformed into the person Christ wants them to be while they heal from their “hurts, habits, and hang-ups”!

More Info

Worship

Worship ministry is a place where people can use their gifts to serve the Lord in the areas of music, video, and more!

More Info

Young Adults

Serving with young adult ministries allows you the opportunity to invest in and disciple young adults during an important season of life.

More Info

Student Ministries

Serving with student ministries allows you the opportunity to invest in and disciple students during an important season of life. 

More Info

Next Steps

Serving within the Next Steps ministries gives us the opportunity to partner with people and help them discover their best next step - from serving, joining community, or being baptized.

More Info

Special Needs Ministry

Serving on the Inclusion Team gives you the opportunity to create accessible, fun, and welcoming services for families with a child who has special needs.

More Info

Pray for others on the weekends in-person after services. Lift up those in need and lend support through prayer. The commitment is just 1 day a week for about 30 minutes or less.
