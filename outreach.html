Local Outreach

Local Outreach Ministries’ primary purpose is to mobilize believers toward a lifestyle of service to help meet the needs of individuals, families, and faith-based organizations in our local community through our Midtown Food Pantry or one of our Local Partners. Our goal is to show the love of Jesus by serving others.

Global Outreach

If you’re open to traveling and want to make a lasting impact on people in need, consider joining one of Northside’s short-term outreach trips. Our goal is to share the hope and love of Jesus Christ through projects such as building houses, providing health care, leading kids’ programs and sports camps, assisting the local church, and more. You don’t have to possess any special skills. Just make yourself available, and you might be surprised what God can do through you when you take the step to Go and Serve.
