PICNICS ARE BACK THIS SUMMER!

Each weekend in June and July, we're making room for connection and summer fun!

We understand how challenging it can be to find meaningful connection with others. Picnic on the Patio is the perfect way to slow down, relax, and connect with others after church. Meet new people, catch up with old friends, hang out with your Life Group, and build relationships! That's what church is for!

Here's what we'll be serving up each weekend for Picnics on the Patio: 

  • June 3 & 4: Free Ice Cream Sandwiches for everyone!
  • June 10 & 11: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each!
  • June 17 & 18: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each! / FREE 💣 Bomb Pops for Dads!
  • June 24 & 25: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each!
  • July 1 & 2: FREE Kona Ice for everyone!
  • July 8 & 9: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each!
  • July 15 & 16: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each!
  • July 22 & 23: Burgers, Hot Dogs, Chips & Soft Drinks / Everything is $1 each!
  • July 29 & 30: TBD
