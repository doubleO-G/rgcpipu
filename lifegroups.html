Groups

Life Change Happens Together

Error

Groups matter because we know that God designed us to grow in relationships with other people. That means God wants to work through other people to help you grow in your faith, and he wants to work through you to help other people grow.

Life groups are a gathering of people meeting weekly. Throughout the year, Life Groups meet over 3 sessions (winter, spring, & fall) with breaks in between. There are groups that meet onsite, offsite, and online. There are groups for men, women, couples, and various seasons of life. Every group strives to study the Bible, share their lives, pray for one another, and encourage next steps of faith.
