THE POWER OF PRAYER Prayer matters. Prayer works. Above all things, prayer is commanded. Colossians 4:2 says "Devote yourselves to prayer, being watchful and thankful." God wants us to come before him and lay out our requests. He also wants us to have other people pray with us. Therefore, Northside has a prayer ministry with the goal of not having people walk alone through difficult challenges. Below you can ask for prayer and someone from our online prayer ministry will get back to you in two business days with an email and prayer. Just click on "Get Prayer".

Need to talk to a pastor now?

Our Pastor-on-Call is available 24/7. Call  (812) 945-8704 and follow the prompts.

NEED PRAYER?

Submit your prayer requests here and we will get back to you with prayer within two business days. Thanks for letting us pray for you!

PRAY WITH OTHERS IN PERSON

After service each week we invite people to stay in their seats if they need prayer. Therefore, we need volunteers to pray with people after each service. This is a once a week, 15 minute commitment, that happens after each of our services. If you love to pray with people we'd love to have you!
