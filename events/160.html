Family Dedication

Family Dedication is for parents wanting to present their child before God and His people, asking for grace and wisdom in carrying out their responsibilities.

We want to celebrate the many children God has blessed us with here at Northside. This will be a time for families to commit to being the true biblical role of parents in their children's lives. Parents have the responsibility to care for the spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being of their children. The most important biblical duty of a parent is to teach their children about Jesus in action and word. Proverbs 22:6 CSB says "Direct your children onto the right path, and when they are older they will not leave it." Learn exactly what all this means at our Family Dedication here at Northside.
