Celebrate Recovery


We all have hurts, habits, and hang-ups. We want you to know that Celebrate Recovery is a place where it’s okay to not be okay. But here’s the cool part—you don’t have to stay that way. God’s radical grace provides not only a way out of the mess, but healing in the midst of it. No matter what you’re facing and no matter what you’re going through, we’re committed to navigating through the tough stuff with you.

We are an ongoing Christ-Centered 12-Step LIFE RECOVERY Ministry to help you find true freedom and meet year round. Celebrate Recovery is also a part of Care Night, which has been designed by the Care Ministry here at Northside Christian Church to promote unity and create community amongst our various care ministries. It is our hope that Care Night will provide a place for all those who are hurting and broken to find not only a place where “It is okay to not be okay”, but also a place to heal, move forward, and thrive in relationship with Christ and with one another.
