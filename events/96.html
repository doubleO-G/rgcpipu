Coffee Talk - Veterans and First Responders

Coffee Talk provides care and community opportunities to those in our community and our Northside family who are veterans, active duty soldiers and officers, and first responders. Coffee Talk is casual convo with other veterans and first responders. Sunday mornings at 10:30 in the West Auditorium.

If you're a veteran, active duty soldier or officer, or first responder, you're invited to enjoy coffee and community with our Military Support Ministry at 10:30AM in the West Auditorium Lobby. It will be a space for prayer, fellowship, and encouragement as we seek to build community among this part of the Northside family.
