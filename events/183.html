Kingdom Conference

Four-day conference for incoming students grades 6-12

Kingdom Conference exists to give every student an opportunity to deepen their relationship with Jesus and others. This is a four-day conference at Northside Christian Church for all incoming students grades 6-12! 


Scholarships can be made available on a case by case basis, please email students@mynorthside.com for more information. 

Details

WHO: Incoming 6th grade students through graduated high school seniors

WHERE: On campus at Northside Christian Church

WHEN: Check in begins at 5:00pm on July 14th. Food will start at 5:00pm each night. Students will need to be picked up from Northside at 10:30pm each night of Kingdom Conference.

Registration cost is currently $45. Kingdom Conference registration will CLOSE on Sunday, July 7th. Email us at students@mynorthside.com if you have questions!
