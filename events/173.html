Party on the Patio

Party on the Patio is an on-site, three-day camp for incoming Kindergarten - 2nd grade students.

Party on the Patio exists to give every student an opportunity to deepen their relationship with Jesus and others. This is a three-day camp at Northside Christian Church for students entering grades Kindergarten - 2nd!

Details

At camp, your child will have experience high-energy worship, fun games, and gather together in small groups to learn about Jesus. Every detail is planned so your child can connect with fellow campers, and their leaders while taking their next steps to follow Jesus. 

Camp is June 24 - 26, 2024 from 9:00am - 12:00pm.
