Next Steps


Next Steps is an environment where people, new to Northside, new to the faith, or looking to get connected in a new way, are invited. This is where you can meet other new people, discover the vision of our church, learn what it means to follow Jesus, and most importantly, take your next step. 

This is a two week experience with brunch provided the first week & lunch provided the second week. Registration is only required once. Children's Ministry services are available during our program. Kids, from 6 weeks old through 5th grade can be checked into class in the Kidside Lobby in the main building. 

If you have questions regarding this event, please contact Shelby Haag, our Next Steps Admin at shaag@mynorthside.com
