Better Together

Better Together is a 2-hour live event for married, engaged, and dating couples of all ages.

Every couple needs a night out to laugh, have fun, and invest in their marriage. Sometimes this is easier said than done. So, we decided to make it easy and planned a date night just for you. We are calling it Better Together, and it even falls right around Valentine’s Day (hint…hint).

Better Together is a 2-hour live event for married, engaged, and dating couples of all ages. Together we will experience a moment of laughter with comedian Marty Simpson. From there, marriage expert, pastor, and author Gary Thomas will join us to equip your marriage through humor and practical steps. Gary is the best-selling author of Sacred Marriage and Cherish and has a heart to encourage marriages and bring people closer to Christ (Learn more HERE). It’s going to be a great night filled with laughter, learning, games, and giveaways alongside the love of your life! 

Details

Logistics: Friday, February 16th from 7:00-9:00pm. There will be Date Night fun from 9:00-10:00pm with desserts, games, and inflatables. Doors open at 6:30pm.  

Seriously Dating/Engaged: Even though this is a marriage focused event, you are welcome to join in as you take next steps towards marriage. 

Cost: $35 per couple.

KidCare: There will not be KidCare at Better Together. Save the date and lock in your babysitters. 

REGISTRATION CLOSES ON FEBRUARY 14TH AT MIDNIGHT!
