Playdate on the Patio

Playdate on the Patio is an afternoon on-site outing for parents and their children under the age of Kindergarten.

Playdate on the Patio is for parents and kiddos ages birth through pre-kindergarten. This event is a fantastic way to build community by meeting other parents while spending time with your child enjoying some fun outdoor activities.

Parents are encouraged to bring sunscreen, towels, lunch, and a friend for this free event.

No registration is necessary.
