Care

In times of transition and recovery, we can all use a guide.

Error

Care Ministry has the honor and responsibility of being the hands and feet of Jesus to our Northside Christian Church family, but also to the greater Kentuckiana community. We want to love and help care for you through life’s difficult transitions and seasons … any struggle that prevents you from living your best life and being your best self. If you don’t know Jesus, or something is preventing you from developing the closeness with Jesus you desire, we can help!

Meet Lori Fortune

Care & Senior Adults Administrative Assistant

Lori began her journey as a Northside staff member in May of 2003. She currently supports the Care Ministry with Care Night through Care Groups, Celebrate Recovery, counseling, weddings, funerals, premarital mentoring, and marriage enrichment. Although she dreams of moving to the beach, Lori has spent most of her life in southern Indiana. She is Mom to Elizabeth and Brendan, and MooMoo to Gabriel. She spends as much of her free time as possible playing in her yard or with her feet up on the front porch.
