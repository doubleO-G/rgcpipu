Celebrate Recovery

Celebrate Recovery

Mondays at 6:30 PM On Site Childcare Available Any Season, Care Group, In-Person, Men, Seniors, Singles, Young Adults, Women
Group Description
We all have hurts, habits, and hang-ups. We want you to know that Celebrate Recovery is a place where its okay to not be okay. But heres the cool partyou dont have to stay that way. Gods radical grace provides not only a way out of the mess, but healing in the midst of it. No matter what youre facing and no matter what youre going through, were committed to navigating through the tough stuff with you. We are an ongoing Christ-Centered 12-Step LIFE RECOVERY Ministry to help you find true freedom and meet year round. Celebrate Recovery is also a part of Care Night, which has been designed by the Care Ministry here at Northside Christian Church to promote unity and create community amongst our various care ministries. It is our hope that Care Night will provide a place for all those who are hurting and broken to find not only a place where It is okay to not be okay, but also a place to heal, move forward, and thrive in relationship with Christ and with one another. To join this group, just click the "Join Group" button and fill out the form, or visit mynorthside.com/care for more information.

DivorceCare - BEGINS MARCH 4, 2024 (Reg & Deb Wassom)

Mondays at 6:30 PM On Site Childcare Available Care Group, In-Person, Men, Singles, Women
Group Description
This session of DivorceCare begins on March 4, 2024. It will meet on Monday nights at 6:30 for 13 weeks. This group can be joined up through the 3rd session on March 18, or until it reaches capacity. After that, you may join the next session which begins on August 26. More details will be emailed before the first group meeting. DivorceCare is provided by Northside Christian Church to provide an encouraging and healthy Christ-centered path through a confusing and difficult time. Youll discover there are people who understand your hurts, emotions, and painful experiences! In divorce, its common to feel isolated. In DivorceCare, youll find that you are not alone, that there are others who understand what you are going through. At Northside we invite you to attend DivorceCare and find the healing and help you have been looking for. DivorceCare is a Care Group that is a part of Care Night. Care Night has been designed by the Care Ministry to promote unity and create community. It is our hope that Care Night will provide a place for all those who are hurting and broken to find not only a place where It is okay to not be okay, but also a place to heal, move forward, and thrive in relationship with Christ and with one another. To join this group, just click the "Join Group" button and fill out the form, or visit mynorthside.com/care for more information. Group curriculum can be purchased at the Resource Center during weekend services or the first night your group meets during Care Night.
